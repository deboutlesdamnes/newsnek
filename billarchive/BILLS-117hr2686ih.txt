 1.  Dolittle Raiders Post Office Building  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 90 Palm Boulevard North in Niceville, Florida, shall be known and designated as the  Doolittle Raiders Post Office Building . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Doolittle Raiders Post Office Building .
