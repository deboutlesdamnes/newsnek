Permanent resident status for Mariana Ndudzi  (a)  In general  Notwithstanding subsections (a) and (b) of section 201 of the Immigration and Nationality Act, Mariana Ndudzi shall be eligible for issuance of an immigrant visa or for adjustment of status to that of an alien lawfully admitted for permanent residence upon filing an application for issuance of an immigrant visa under section 204 of such Act or for adjustment of status to lawful permanent resident.
