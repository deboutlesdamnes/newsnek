 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Extending Temporary Emergency Scheduling of Fentanyl Analogues Act .
 2.  Extension of temporary order for fentanyl-related substances Effective as if included in the enactment of the Temporary Reauthorization and Study of the Emergency Scheduling of Fentanyl Analogues Act ( Public Law 116–114 ), section 2 of such Act ( Public Law 116–114 ) is amended by striking  May 6, 2021  and inserting  September 30, 2021 .
