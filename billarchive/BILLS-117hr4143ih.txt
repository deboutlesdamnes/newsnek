 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Know Your Social Security Act .
 2.  Clarification of requirement to mail social security account statements  (a)  In general Section 1143 of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1320b–13 ) is amended— (1) in subsection (a)(1), by adding at the end the following:  Such statement shall be provided by mail unless the requesting individual chooses electronic delivery for that request. ; and (2)  in subsection (c)(2)—  (A) by striking  Beginning not later than  and inserting  (A) Beginning not later than ;  (B) by inserting  by mail  after  provide ; and (C)  by adding at the end the following:  (B)  In any case in which an eligible individual described in subparagraph (A) responds to an annual inquiry by the Commissioner relating to the mailing of the individual’s statement by making an election that such statement for such year be provided in electronic form only, the requirements of this paragraph shall be deemed to be satisfied for such year with respect to the individual.  .  (b)  Effective date  The amendments made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to social security account statements required to be provided after the date of the enactment of this Act.
