
 1.  Designation The Federal building located at 1200 New Jersey Avenue Southeast in Washington, DC, shall be known and designated as the  Norman Yoshio Mineta Federal Building .
  2.  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Federal building referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Norman Yoshio Mineta Federal Building .
