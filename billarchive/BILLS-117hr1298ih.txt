 1.  Staff Sergeant Marshal Roberts Post Office Building  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1233 North Cedar Street in Owasso, Oklahoma, shall be known and designated as the  Staff Sergeant Marshal Roberts Post Office Building . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Staff Sergeant Marshal Roberts Post Office Building .
