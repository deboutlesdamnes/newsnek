 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Plasma Donation Awareness Act of 2021 . 
 2.  Importance of the blood and plasma supply  (a)  In general Section 3226 of the CARES Act ( Public Law 116–136 ) is amended— (1) in the section heading, by inserting  and plasma  after  blood ; and (2) by inserting after  blood  each time it appears  and plasma . (b)  Conforming amendment The item relating to section 3226 in the table of contents in section 2 of the CARES Act ( Public Law 116–136 ) is amended to read as follows: Sec. 3226. Importance of the blood and plasma supply.  .
