 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Washington Channel Public Access Act .
 2.  Prohibition on implementation of rule The Secretary of the Army may not finalize, implement, or enforce the proposed rule entitled  Washington Channel, Fort McNair, Washington, DC; Restricted Area , published by the Department of the Army on December 29, 2020 (Docket No. COE–2019–0010; 85 Fed. Reg. 85570), or any other rule that restricts public access to the Washington Channel in Washington, DC.
