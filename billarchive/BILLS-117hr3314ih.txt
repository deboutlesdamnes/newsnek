 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  No Land Grab Act of 2021 .
 2.  Protection of private property  (a)  In general  Section 216 of Executive Order 14008 (86 Fed. Reg. 7619; relating to Tackling the Climate Crisis at Home and Abroad) shall not have the force and effect of law.  (b)  Recommendations of report  No Federal agency may take an action if such action is solely authorized by a report or recommendation issued pursuant to such section.
