 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  FEMA Emergency Management Performance Grants Program Reauthorization Act of 2021 .
 2.  Authorization of emergency management performance grants Section 662(f) of the Post-Katrina Emergency Management Reform Act of 2006 ( 6 U.S.C. 762(f) ) is amended by striking  2018 through 2022  and inserting  2022 through 2026 .
