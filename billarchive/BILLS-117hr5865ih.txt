 1.  Leonard Scarcella Post Office Building  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 4110 Bluebonnet Drive in Stafford, Texas, shall be known and designated as the  Leonard Scarcella Post Office Building . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Leonard Scarcella Post Office Building .
