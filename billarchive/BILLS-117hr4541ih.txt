 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Responding Aggressively to Prevent Insects and Disease Act  or the  RAPID Act .
 2.  Clarification of existing categorical exclusion authority related to insect and disease infestation  (a)  Insect and disease categorical exclusion Section 603(c) of the Healthy Forests Restoration Act of 2003 ( 16 U.S.C. 6591b(c) ) is amended— (1) in paragraph (1), by striking  3000 acres  and inserting  10,000 acres ; and (2) in paragraph (2)(B), by striking  Fire Regime Groups I, II, or III,  and inserting  Fire Regime I, Fire Regime II, Fire Regime III, Fire Regime IV, or Fire Regime V, .
 3.  Clarification of existing categorical exclusion authority related to wildfire resilience projects Section 605(c) of the Healthy Forests Restoration Act of 2003 ( 16 U.S.C. 6591d(c) ) is amended— (1) in paragraph (1), by striking  3000 acres  and inserting  10,000 acres ; and (2) in paragraph (2)(B), by striking  Fire Regime Groups I, II, or III  and inserting  Fire Regime I, Fire Regime II, Fire Regime III, Fire Regime IV, or Fire Regime V .
