
 1.  Officer Eric H. Talley Post Office Building 
 (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1905 15th Street in Boulder, Colorado, shall be known and designated as the  Officer Eric H. Talley Post Office Building .  (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Officer Eric H. Talley Post Office Building .
