 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Defend our Coast Act .
 2.  Withdrawal from disposition of Mid-Atlantic outer Continental Shelf Section 12 of the Outer Continental Shelf Lands Act ( 43 U.S.C. 1341 ) is amended by adding at the end the following: (g)  Withdrawal from disposition of Mid-Atlantic outer Continental Shelf  The Secretary may not issue a lease for the exploration, development, or production of oil or gas on the outer Continental Shelf in the Mid-Atlantic planning area.  .
