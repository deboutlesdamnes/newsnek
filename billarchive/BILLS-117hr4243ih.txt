 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Improving Capital Allocation for Newcomers Act of 2021 .
 2.  Qualifying venture capital funds Section 3(c)(1) of the Investment Company Act of 1940 ( 15 U.S.C. 80a–3(c)(1) ) is amended— (1) in the matter preceding subparagraph (A), by striking  250 persons  and inserting  600 persons ; and (2) in subparagraph (C)(i), by striking  $10,000,000  and inserting  $150,000,000 .
