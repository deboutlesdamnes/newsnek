
 1.  Patsy Cline Post Office 
 (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 132 North Loudoun Street, Suite 1 in Winchester, Virginia, shall be known and designated as the  Patsy Cline Post Office .  (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Patsy Cline Post Office .
