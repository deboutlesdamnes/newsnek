
 1.  W.O.C. Kort Miller Plantenberg Post Office 
 (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 317 Blattner Drive in Avon, Minnesota, shall be known and designated as the  W.O.C. Kort Miller Plantenberg Post Office .  (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  W.O.C. Kort Miller Plantenberg Post Office .
