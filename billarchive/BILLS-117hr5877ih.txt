 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Pension Risk Transfer Accountability Act of 2021 . 
 2.  Review of Pension Risk Transfer Interpretive Bulletin  Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Labor shall—  (1)  review section 2509.95–1 of title 29, Code of Federal Regulations (relating to the fiduciary standards under the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 when selecting an annuity provider for a defined benefit pension plan) to determine whether amendments to such section are warranted; and  (2)  report to Congress on the findings of such review, including an assessment of any risk to participants.
