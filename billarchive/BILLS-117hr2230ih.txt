
 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Delivering Envelopes Judiciously On-time Year-round Act  or the  DEJOY Act .
  2.  Service standards for first-class mail  Section 3691 of title 39, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following: 

 (e)  Service standards for first-Class mail  Notwithstanding any other provision of this title, the service standards for first-class mail shall be such standards as in effect on January 1, 2021.  .
