 1.  Ronald A. Robinson Post Office  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 5420 Kavanaugh Boulevard in Little Rock, Arkansas, shall be known and designated as the  Ronald A. Robinson Post Office . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Ronald A. Robinson Post Office .
