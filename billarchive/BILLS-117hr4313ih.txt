 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Small Refinery Exemption Clarification Act of 2021 .
 2.  Clarification of eligibility of small refineries for an exemption from requirements of the Renewable Fuel Program Notwithstanding any other provision of law, a small refinery may petition under section 211(o)(9) of the Clean Air Act ( 42 U.S.C. 7545(o)(9) ) for an exemption from the requirements of section 211(o)(2) of such Act ( 42 U.S.C. 7545(o)(2) ) only if that refinery has continuously received an exemption from such requirements every year since 2011.
