 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  FEMA Relief Extension Act .
 2.  Period of assistance extension  (a)  Stafford Act Clauses (iii) and (iv) of section 408(c)(1)(B) of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act are amended by striking  18-month period  and inserting  24-month period . (b)  Regulation  The Administrator of the Federal Emergency Management Agency shall amend section 206.110(e) of title 44, Code of Federal Regulations, to comply with the amendment made by subsection (a).
