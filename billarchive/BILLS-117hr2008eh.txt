
 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Local Water Protection Act .
  2.  Nonpoint source management programs Section 319(j) of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act ( 33 U.S.C. 1329(j) ) is amended by striking  subsections (h) and (i) not to exceed  and all that follows through  fiscal year 1991  and inserting  subsections (h) and (i) $200,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2022 through 2026 .
