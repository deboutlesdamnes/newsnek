 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Science-Based Emissions Standards Act of 2021 .
 2.  Ensuring completion of marginal well study prior to replacement methane rules  (a)  In general The Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency may not finalize any rule to amend the new source performance standards under section 111 of the Clean Air Act ( 42 U.S.C. 7411 ) (including the regulations thereunder, including subpart OOOOa of part 60 of title 40, Code of Federal Regulations), to reduce methane emissions from new and modified oil and natural gas facilities until the Secretary of Energy— (1)  completes and publishes the results of the study titled  Quantification of Methane Emissions from Marginal (Small Producing) Oil and Gas Wells  (project number DE–FE0031702); and (2)  provides a period of at least 90 days for public comment on the application of the results of such study to inform such rule.  (b)  Consideration of marginal well study  In finalizing any rule described in subsection (a), the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency shall take into consideration the results of such study.
