 1.  Gary James Fletcher Post Office Building  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1304 4th Avenue in Canyon, Texas, shall be known and designated as the  Gary James Fletcher Post Office Building . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Gary James Fletcher Post Office Building .
