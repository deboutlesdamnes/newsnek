 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Taxpayer Penalty Protection Act of 2021 .
 2.  Temporary safe harbor for failure by individuals to pay estimated income tax  In the case of any taxable year beginning in 2020—  (1)  In general  Section 6654(d)(1)(B)  of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 shall be applied— (A) by substituting  70 percent  for  90 percent  each place such term appears in clause (i) of such section, and (B) by substituting  70 percent  for  100 percent  in clause (ii) of such section. (2) Individuals with adjusted gross income in excess of  $150,000 Section 6654(d)(1)(C)(i) of such Code shall be applied by substituting  90 percent  for  110 percent  (and without regard to the substitution made by paragraph (1)(B) of this section). (3)  Farmers and fishermen  Section 6654(i) of such Code shall be applied without regard to the substitutions made by this section.
