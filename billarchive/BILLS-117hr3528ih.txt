 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Warren Cowles Grade Crossing Safety Act .
 2.  Authorization of appropriations for highway-rail grade crossing improvement projects  Section 22907 of title 49, United States Code, is amended—  (1) in subsection (c)(5), by inserting  , including such a project for commuter rail and operators in high-ridership corridors  before the period at the end; and (2)  by adding at the end the following:  (m)  Authorization of appropriations  (1)  In general  Notwithstanding section 22905(f), there are authorized to be appropriated for eligible projects described in subsection (c)(5) for commuter rail and operators in high-ridership corridors—  (A)  for fiscal year 2022, $250,000,000; and  (B)  for fiscal year 2023 and each fiscal year thereafter, the amount described in paragraph (2).  (2)  Amount described  The amount described in this paragraph for a fiscal year is $250,000,000, adjusted to reflect the percentage (if any) of the increase in the average of the Consumer Price Index for the preceding 12-month period compared to the Consumer Price Index for fiscal year 2021.  (3)  Consumer price index defined In this subsection, the term  Consumer Price Index  means the Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers published by the Bureau of Labor Statistics of the Department of Labor. .
