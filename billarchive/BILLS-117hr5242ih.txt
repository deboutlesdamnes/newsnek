 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Supporting Teachers with Residency Opportunities and New Grants Act of 2021  or the  STRONG Act of 2021  .
 2.  Teacher residency programs  (a)  Partnership grants Section 202(e)(2)(B)(i)(I) of the Higher Education Act of 1965 ( 20 U.S.C. 1022a(e)(2)(B)(i)(I) ) is amended— (1) by striking  education or  and inserting  education, ; and (2) by striking  accomplishment  and inserting  accomplishment, or enrolled in a pre-baccalaureate program at a four-year institution of higher education with an academic major relating to teaching . (b)  Public service loan forgiveness Section 455(m)(3)(B) of the Higher Education Act of 1965 ( 20 U.S.C. 1087e(m)(3)(B) ) is amended— (1) in clause (i), by striking  or  at the end; (2) in clause (ii), by striking the period at the end and inserting  ; or ; and (3)  by adding at the end the following:  (iii)  participating, as a pre-baccalaureate student, in a teacher residency program under section 202 of this Act.  .   (c)  Authorization of appropriations Section 209 of the Higher Education Act of 1965 ( 20 U.S.C. 1022h ) is amended by striking  $300,000,000  and all that follows and inserting  $600,000,000 for fiscal years 2022 through 2024 and such sums as may be necessary for each succeeding fiscal year. .
