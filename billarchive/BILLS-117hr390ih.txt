 1.  Redesignation The Federal building located at 167 North Main Street in Memphis, Tennessee, commonly known as the  Clifford Davis and Odell Horton Federal Building , shall be known and designated as the  Odell Horton Federal Building .
 2.  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Federal building referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Odell Horton Federal Building .
