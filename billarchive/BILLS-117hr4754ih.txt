 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  National Statuary Hall Collection Progress Assurance Act . 
 2. (b)  Effective date  The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to requests which are forwarded to the Joint Committee on the Library by the Architect of the Capitol on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
