 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Eliminating Foreign Intervention in Elections Act .
 2.  Federal payment restriction  (a)  In general  None of the funds made available in any Act may be used to make payments to any unit of local government that permits individuals who are not citizens of the United States to vote in elections for State or local office.  (b)  Publication of information by Census required  The Bureau of the Census shall collect and publish information on any State or local government that allows non-citizens to vote.  (c)  Applicability  The requirements of this section shall only apply with respect to any contract entered into or grant made on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
