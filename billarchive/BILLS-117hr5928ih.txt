
 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  VA Canine Reporting Act of 2021 .
  2.  Report on Service dogs; guide dogs  Section 1714 of title 38, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection: 

 (g)  Not less than annually, the Secretary shall submit to the Committees on Veterans’ Affairs of the Senate and House of Representatives a report, including—   (1)  the average time to provide a veteran with a service dog or a guide dog in the prior year; and 
 (2)  legislative recommendations of the Secretary to reduce the wait time for a veteran to be provided with a service dog or a guide dog.  .
