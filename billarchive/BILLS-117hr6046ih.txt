
 1.  Passenger ferry grants  Section 5307(h) of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:    (4)  Passenger ferries in nonurbanized areas  Notwithstanding paragraphs (1) and (2) and subsection (a), the Secretary may also make grants under this subsection to passenger ferry projects in nonurbanized areas regardless of population size. Such grants shall be subject to the requirements of section 5311 of title 49, United States Code.  .
