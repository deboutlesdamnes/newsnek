 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Student Loan Interest Deduction Act of 2021 .
 2.  Increase in deduction for student loan interest  (a)  Increase in dollar limitation and repeal of limitation based on income  Section 221(b)  of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended to read as follows: (b)  Maximum deduction  The deduction allowed by subsection (a) for the taxable year shall not exceed $5,000 ($10,000 in the case of a joint return).  .  (b)  Conforming amendments  Section 221 of such Code is amended by striking subsections (e) and (f) (relating to special rules and inflation adjustments, respectively) and inserting the following new subsection:  (e)  Denial of double benefit  No deduction shall be allowed under this section for any amount for which a deduction is allowable under any other provision of this chapter.  .  (c)  Effective date  The amendments made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act.
