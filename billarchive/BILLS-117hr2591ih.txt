 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Dispose Unused Medications and Prescription Opioids Act  or the  DUMP Opioids Act . 
 2.  Requirement for certain department of veterans affairs medical facilities to have physical location for the disposal of controlled substances medications  (a)  In general  The Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall ensure that each covered Department medical facility has a physical location where any individual may dispose of controlled substances medications.  (b)  Covered department medical facility defined In this section, the term  covered Department medical facility  means a medical facility of the Department of Veterans Affairs with an onsite pharmacy or a physical location dedicated for law enforcement purposes. (c)  Effective date  This section shall take effect on January 1, 2022.
