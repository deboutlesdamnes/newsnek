 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Contact Lens Prescription Verification Modernization Act .
 2.  Amendments Section 4 of the Fairness to Contact Lens Consumers Act ( 15 U.S.C. 7603 ) is amended— (1)  in subsection (a)—  (A)  by redesignating paragraphs (1) and (2) as subparagraphs (A) and (B), respectively;  (B)  by moving such redesignated subparagraphs 2 ems to the right;  (C) by striking  A seller may  and inserting (1)  Sellers generally. —A seller may; and (D)  by adding at the end the following new paragraph:  (2)  Online sellers An online seller of contact lenses shall provide a method that enables an individual to electronically transmit, in accordance with HIPAA privacy regulation (as defined in section 1180(b)(3) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1320d–9(b)(3) )), a copy of a contact lens prescription for such individual.
