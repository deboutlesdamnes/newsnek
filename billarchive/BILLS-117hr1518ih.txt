 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  21st Century NICS Act .
 2.  Requirement that the NICS system search the National Data Exchange database in conducting background checks Section 103(e)(1) of the Brady Handgun Violence Prevention Act ( 34 U.S.C. 40901(e)(1) ) is amended by adding at the end the following: (L)  Search of National Data Exchange database  The system established under this section may search the database of the National Data Exchange in conducting a background check under this section.  .
