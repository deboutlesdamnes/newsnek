 2.  Telephone hours at Social Security Administration offices and certain State offices  (a)  In general Section 205 of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 405 ) is amended by adding at the end the following: (v)  Telephone hours at Social Security Administration offices  The Commissioner of Social Security shall ensure that each office of the Social Security Administration and each State agency that makes disability determinations as described in section 221(a)(1) is fully staffed with employees to answer calls during standard business hours, including 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. local time, Mondays, Tuesdays, Wednesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays, excluding Federal holidays.   .  (b)  Effective date  The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect on January 1, 2023.
