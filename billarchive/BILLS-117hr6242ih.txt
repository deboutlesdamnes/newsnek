 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Family Unity Act of 2021 .
 2.  Narrowing of limitations on the scope of judicial review of certain removal actions Section 242(g) of the Immigration and Nationality Act ( 8 U.S.C. 1252(g) ) is amended by striking  or claim by or on behalf of any alien arising from the decision or action  and inserting  or claim by or on behalf of any alien challenging the discretionary decision or action .
