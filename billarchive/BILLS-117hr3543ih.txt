 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Blockchain Technology Coordination Act of 2021 .
 2.  Establishment of national blockchain technology coordination office There is established within the Department of Commerce a National Blockchain Technology Coordination Office (in this Act referred to as the  Office ) which shall be organized and led by a Director, who shall be appointed by the Secretary not later than 60 days after the date of enactment of this Act.
