 1.  Private First Class Barrett Lyle Austin Post Office Building  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 810 South Pendleton Street in Easley, South Carolina, shall be known and designated as the  Private First Class Barrett Lyle Austin Post Office Building . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Private First Class Barrett Lyle Austin Post Office Building .
