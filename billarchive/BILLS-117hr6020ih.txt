
 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Supporting Medicare Providers Act of 2021 .
  2.  Extending certain increases in payments for physicians’ services under the Medicare program through 2022   (a)  In general Section 1848(t) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1395w–4(t) ) is amended—
 (1)  in paragraph (1)—   (A) by striking  during 2021  and inserting  during 2021 or 2022 ; and
 (B) by striking  January 1, 2022  and inserting  January 1, 2023 ; and  (2)  in paragraph (2)(C)— 
 (A) in the header, by striking  2021  and inserting  2021 and 2022 ; and  (B) by striking  after 2021  and inserting  after 2022 .
 (b)  Conforming amendment Section 1848(c)(2)(B)(iv)(V) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1395w–4(c)(2)(B)(iv)(V) ) is amended by striking  2021  and inserting  2021 or 2022 .
