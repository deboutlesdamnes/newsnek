 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Urban Indian Health Parity Act . 
 2.  Full Federal medical assistance percentage for urban Indian organizations Section 1905(b) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1396d(b) ) is amended by inserting after  by an Indian tribe or tribal organization (as defined in section 4 of the Indian Health Care Improvement Act)  the following:  , or through an urban Indian organization (as defined in section 4 of such Act) pursuant to a grant or contract with the Indian Health Service under title V of the Indian Health Care Improvement Act .
