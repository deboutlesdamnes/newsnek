 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Infants Need Food And Nutrition Today Act  or the  INFANT Act .
 2.  Infant food combinations and dinners  (a)  In general Not later than 30 days after the date of the enactment of this section, the Secretary of Agriculture shall allow infant food combinations and dinners to be eligible to be served under the supplemental nutrition program for women, infants, and children under section 17 of the Child Nutrition Act of 1966 ( 42 U.S.C. 1786 ). (b)  Revision of regulations  The Secretary of Agriculture shall issue and revise regulations in accordance with subsection (a).
