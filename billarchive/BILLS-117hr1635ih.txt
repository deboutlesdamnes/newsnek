 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  STOP II: Classrooms Over Conference Rooms Act .
 2.  Grant program for school security  (a)  In general Section 2705 of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 ( 34 U.S.C. 10555 ) is amended— (1)  by amending subsection (a) to read as follows:  (a)  In general  There are authorized to be appropriated $200,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2022 through 2028, of which, for each fiscal year—  (1)  $133,333,333 shall be made available to the BJA Director to carry out this part; and  (2)  $66,666,667 shall be made available to the COPS Director to carry out this part.  ; and  (2) in subsection (b) by adding at the end the following:  Any funds appropriated under the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2021, which may be used for purposes of renting conference rooms in the District of Columbia in carrying out the Department of Education Organization Act shall instead be used for the purposes in subsection (a). . (b)  Authorization  There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out the Department of Education Organization Act, $270,000,000 (reduced by $270,000,000) for fiscal year 2022.
