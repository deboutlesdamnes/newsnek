 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Honoring Our Fallen TSA Officers Act .
 2.  Transportation Security Administration employees eligible to receive public safety officers’ death benefits Section 1204(9) of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 ( 34 U.S.C. 10284(9) ) is amended— (1) in subparagraph (C), by striking  or  at the end; (2) in subparagraph (D), by striking the period at the end and inserting  ; or ; and (3)  by inserting after subparagraph (D) the following:  (E)  an employee of the Transportation Security Administration who is performing official duties of the Administration, if those official duties are related to protecting the Nation's transportation systems to ensure freedom of movement for people and commerce.  . 
 3.  Effective date  This Act and the amendments made by this Act shall take effect as if they had been enacted on October 31, 2013.
