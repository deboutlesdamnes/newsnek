 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Diversity and Inclusion Data Accountability and Transparency Act . 
 2.  Disclosures by regulated entities Section 342(b) of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act ( 12 U.S.C. 5452(b) ) is amended by adding at the end the following: (5)  Disclosures by regulated entities  The Director of each Office shall require entities with 100 employees or greater regulated by the applicable agency to provide such information as may be required to carry out the duties of the Director.  .
