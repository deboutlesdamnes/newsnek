
 1.  Robert S. McKeithen Post Office Building 
 (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 135 Main Street in Biloxi, Mississippi, shall be known and designated as the  Robert S. McKeithen Post Office Building .  (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Robert S. McKeithen Post Office Building .
