 1.  Norma Comnick Post Office Building  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 2141 Ferry Street in Anderson, California, shall be known and designated as the  Norma Comnick Post Office Building . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Norma Comnick Post Office Building .
