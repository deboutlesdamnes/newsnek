 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Preserving Direct Seller Independence Act .
 2.  Definitions Section 3(e) of the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 ( 29 U.S.C. 203(e) ) is amended by inserting after paragraph (5) the following: (6) The term  employee  does not include any person identified in  section 3508(b)(2)  of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986. .
