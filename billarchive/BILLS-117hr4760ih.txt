 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Criminal Alien Removal Clarification Act of 2021 .
 2.  Criminal grounds for deportability Section 237(a)(2) of the Immigration and Nationality Act ( 8 U.S.C. 1227(a)(2) ) is amended to read as follows: (2)  Criminal grounds  Any alien who, at any time after admission, has been convicted of a felony or two misdemeanors, whether under State or Federal law, is deportable.  .
