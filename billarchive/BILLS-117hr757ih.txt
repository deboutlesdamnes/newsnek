Exemption of New Mexico from certain provisions of certain orders related to oil and gas drilling  The following provisions shall not apply to the State of New Mexico:  (1)  Section 208 of Executive Order 14008 on Tackling the Climate Crisis at Home and Abroad, issued on January 27, 2021 (relating to a pause on new oil and natural gas leases on public lands).
