 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Make the Migrant Protection Protocols Mandatory Act of 2021 .
 2.  Mandatory implementation of the Migrant Protection Protocols Section 235(b)(2)(C) of the Immigration and Nationality Act ( 8 U.S.C. 1225(b)(2)(C) ) is amended by striking  may  and inserting  shall .
