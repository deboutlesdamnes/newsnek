
 1.  Benjamin A. Gilman Post Office Building 
 (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 40 Fulton Street in Middletown, New York, shall be known and designated as the  Benjamin A. Gilman Post Office Building .  (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Benjamin A. Gilman Post Office Building .
