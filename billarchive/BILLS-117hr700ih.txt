 1. Lawrence M.  Larry  Walsh Sr. Post Office (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 303 East Mississippi Avenue in Elwood, Illinois, shall be known and designated as the Lawrence M.  Larry  Walsh Sr. Post Office. (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Lawrence M.  Larry  Walsh Sr. Post Office.
