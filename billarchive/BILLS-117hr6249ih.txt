 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  End Polluter Welfare for Enhanced Oil Recovery Act of 2021 .
 2.  Elimination of use of carbon oxide as tertiary injectant  (a)  In general  Section 45Q(f)  of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:  (8)  Elimination of use of carbon oxide as tertiary injectant In the case of any qualified facility the construction of which begins after the date of the enactment of the  End Polluter Welfare for Enhanced Oil Recovery Act of 2021 , subsection (a)(4)(B)(i) shall not apply. .  (b)  Effective date  The amendments made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act. 
 3.  Enhanced oil recovery credit  (a)  In general Subpart D of part IV of subchapter A of  chapter 1  of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking section 43.
