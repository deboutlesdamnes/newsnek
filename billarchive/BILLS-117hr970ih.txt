 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Opportunity Zone Extension Act of 2021 . 
 2.  Extension of election and gain deferral periods until December 31, 2028  (a)  Extension of election period  Section 1400Z–2(a)(2)(B)  of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking  December 31, 2026  and inserting  December 31, 2028 .  (b)  Extension of deferral period Section 1400Z–2(b)(1)(B) of such Code is amended by striking  December 31, 2026  and inserting  December 31, 2028 . (c)  Effective date The amendments made by this section shall apply as if included in the enactment of section 13823 of  Public Law 115–97 .
