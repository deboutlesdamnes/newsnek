 1.  Short title; findings  (a)  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Physical Therapist Workforce and Patient Access Act of 2021 . (b)  Findings  The Congress finds as follows:  (1)  Physical therapists play an important role in the prevention, treatment, or management of pain for individuals, including those with substance use disorders, or at risk of developing a substance use disorder.  (2)  Physical therapists are also playing an important role in the physical rehabilitation needs of individuals who have developed chronic health conditions as a result of COVID–19.
