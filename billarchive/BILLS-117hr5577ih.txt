 1.  John R. Lewis Post Office Building  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 3900 Crown Road Southwest in Atlanta, Georgia, shall be known and designated as the  John R. Lewis Post Office Building . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  John R. Lewis Post Office Building .
