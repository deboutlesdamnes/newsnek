
 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Repealing Existing Substandard Provisions Encouraging Conciliation with Tribes Act .
  2.  Repeal of certain obsolete laws relating to Indians   (1) Section 2080 of the Revised Statutes ( 25 U.S.C. 72 ) is repealed.
