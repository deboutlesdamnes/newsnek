
 1.  Charles P. Nord Post Office 
 (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 100 3rd Avenue Northwest in Perham, Minnesota, shall be known and designated as the  Charles P. Nord Post Office .  (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Charles P. Nord Post Office .
