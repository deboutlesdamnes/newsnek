 1.  Designation of John R. Lewis Voting Rights Highway  (a)  Designation The portion of United States Route 80 from Selma, Alabama to Montgomery, Alabama shall be known as the  John R. Lewis Voting Rights Highway . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the portion of United States Route 80 from Selma, Alabama to Montgomery, Alabama is deemed to be a reference to the  John R. Lewis Voting Rights Highway .
