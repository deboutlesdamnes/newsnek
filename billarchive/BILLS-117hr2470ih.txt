(d)  Operation of vehicles  Section 127(l)(3)(A) of title 23, United States Code, is amended—  (1) in the matter preceding clause (i) by striking  clauses (i) through (iv)  and inserting  clauses (i) through (v) ; and (2)  by adding at the end the following:  (v)  The Louie B.
