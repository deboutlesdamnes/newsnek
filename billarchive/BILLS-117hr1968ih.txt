 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Puerto Rico Insurance Excise Tax Exemption Act of 2021 .
 2.  Certain insurance policies issued by United States territory and possession insurers exempt from foreign insurer excise tax  (a)  In general  Section 4373  of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph: (3)  Territories and possessions  Any policy of insurance, indemnity bond, annuity contract, or policy of reinsurance issued by a partnership or corporation created or organized under the laws of any territory or possession of the United States, unless any of the hazards, risks, losses, or liabilities covered thereby are covered by a policy of reinsurance issued by a foreign insurer or reinsurer (other than such a partnership or corporation).  .  (b)  Effective date  The amendments made by this section shall apply to premiums paid after the date of the enactment of this Act.
