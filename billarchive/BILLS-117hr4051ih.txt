 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Opioid Treatment Providers Act .
 2.  Eligibility of opioid treatment programs for health professions opportunity grants Section 2008(a)(4)(A) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1397g(a)(4)(A) ) is amended by striking  or a community-based organization  and inserting  , a community-based organization, or an opioid treatment program (as defined in section 1861(jjj)(2)) .
 3.  Effective date  The amendment made by this Act shall take effect on October 1, 2021.
