 1.  Designation The Federal Building and United States Courthouse located at 111 North Adams Street in Tallahassee, Florida, shall be known and designated as the  Joseph Woodrow Hatchett United States Courthouse and Federal Building .
 2.  Reference Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Federal Building and United States Courthouse referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Joseph Woodrow Hatchett United States Courthouse and Federal Building .
