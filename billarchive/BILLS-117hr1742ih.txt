 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Real Estate Exchange Fairness Act of 2021 .
 2.  Puerto Rico treated as part of United States for purposes of like-kind exchanges  (a)  In general  Section 1031(h)  of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following: For purposes of the preceding sentence, the term  United States  shall be treated as including Puerto Rico.. (b)  Effective date  The amendment made by this section shall apply to exchanges completed after the date of the enactment of this Act.
