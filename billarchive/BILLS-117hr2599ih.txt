 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Sandy Duplication of Benefits Fairness Act .
 2.  Duplication of benefits  (a)  Amendment Section 1210(a)(3) of the FAA Reauthorization Act of 2018 ( Public Law 115–254 ; 132 Stat. 3186) is amended by striking  January 1, 2016  and inserting  October 1, 2012 . (b)  Applicability The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect as if included in the enactment of the FAA Reauthorization Act of 2018 ( Public Law 115–254 ; 132 Stat. 3186).
