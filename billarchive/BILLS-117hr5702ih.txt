 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Preventing the Loss of American Jobs due to Unjust Mandates Act .
 2.  Prohibition on certain work authorizations  Effective beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act, all Federal agencies are prohibited from granting a work authorization to an alien for the purpose of effectively replacing any unvaccinated U.S. healthcare worker terminated from their position as a result of a local, State, or Federal vaccine mandate.
