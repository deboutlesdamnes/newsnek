Training and technical assistance  (a)  In general  The Secretary shall provide training and technical assistance to recipients of grants under sections 2011 and 2013 of this title and recipients of per diem payments under section 2012 of this title regarding the planning, development, and provision of services for which the grant or payment is made.  (b)  Provision of training and technical assistance  The Secretary may provide training and technical assistance under paragraph (1) directly or through grants or contracts with such public or nonprofit private entities as the Secretary considers appropriate.
