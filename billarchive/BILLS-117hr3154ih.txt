 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Building on Reemployment Improvements to Deliver Good Employment (BRIDGE) for Workers Act .
 2.  Eligibility for reemployment services  (a)  CARES Act  (1)  Pandemic Unemployment Assistance Section 2102 of the Cares Act ( Public Law 116–136 ) is amended by adding at the end the following: (i)  Eligibility for reemployment services Pandemic unemployment assistance under this section shall be treated as unemployment compensation for the purposes of section 306 of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 506 ). .  (2)  Pandemic Emergency Unemployment Compensation Section 2107 of the Cares Act ( Public Law 116–136 ) is amended by adding at the end the following: (h)  Eligibility for reemployment services Pandemic emergency unemployment compensation under this section shall be treated as unemployment compensation for the purposes of section 306 of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 506 ). .  (b)  Social Security Act Section 306(a) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 506(a) ) is amended— (1) by striking  individuals referred to reemployment services as described in section 303(j)  and inserting  claimants for unemployment compensation, including claimants referred to reemployment services as described in section 303(j), ; and (2) by striking  such individuals  and inserting  such claimants .
