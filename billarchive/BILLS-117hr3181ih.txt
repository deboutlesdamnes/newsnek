 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Health and Human Services Hiree Clarification Act .
 2.  Hiring authority restricted The Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency may not hire or pay the salary or expense of any officer or employee of the Environmental Protection Agency under subsection (f) or (g) of section 207 of the Public Health Service Act ( 42 U.S.C. 209 ) who is not already receiving pay under either such subsection on the date of the enactment of this Act.
