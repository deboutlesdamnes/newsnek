
 1.  Dr. C.T. Wright Post Office Building 
 (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 16605 East Avenue of the Fountains in Fountain Hills, Arizona, shall be known and designated as the  Dr. C.T. Wright Post Office Building .  (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Dr. C.T. Wright Post Office Building .
