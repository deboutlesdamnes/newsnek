 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  States Have A Say Act .
 2.  State approval required for resettlement of refugees Section 412 of the Immigration and Nationality Act ( 8 U.S.C. 1522 ) is amended by adding at the end the following:  (g)  State approval  Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Director may not resettle a refugee in a State if the Governor of that State does not consent to such resettlement.   .
