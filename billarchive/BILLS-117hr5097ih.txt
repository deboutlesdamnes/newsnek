 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Veterans Equitable Taxation of Student Loans Act  or the  V.E.T. Student Loans Act .
 2.  Exclusion of discharged student loan debt from gross income of veterans  (a)  In general  Section 108(f)  of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:  (6)  Special rule for Veterans In the case of a taxpayer who is a veteran of the Armed Forces of the United States, paragraph (5) shall be applied by substituting  after December 31, 2000  for  after December 31, 2020, and before January 1, 2026 .  .  (b)  Effective date  The amendment made by this section shall take effect as if included in section 9675 of the American Rescue Plan Act of 2021.
