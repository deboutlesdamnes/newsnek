 1.  Extension of covered period for paycheck protection program  (a)  In general Section 7(a)(36)(A)(iii) of the Small Business Act ( 15 U.S.C. 636(a)(36)(A)(iii) ) is amended by striking  March 31, 2021  and inserting  June 30, 2021 . (b)  Funding Section 1102(b)(1) of the CARES Act ( Public Law 116–136 ), as amended by section 323 of the Economic Aid to Hard-Hit Small Businesses, Nonprofits, and Venues Act ( Public Law 116–260 ), is amended by striking  March 31, 2021  and inserting  June 30, 2021 .
