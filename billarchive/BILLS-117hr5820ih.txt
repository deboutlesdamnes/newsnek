 1.  Prohibiting use of Federal funds to facilitate mandatory vaccination programs  (a)  In general  Federal funds made available to a State, or a political subdivision thereof, may not be used, directly or indirectly, to facilitate a COVID–19 vaccine mandate of such State or political subdivision.  (b)  Definitions  In this section:  (1)  COVID–19 vaccine The term  COVID–19 vaccine  means a vaccine that is licensed under section 351 of the Public Health Service Act ( 42 U.S.C. 262 ) or authorized for emergency use under section 564 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act ( 21 U.S.C. 360bbb–3 ) for COVID–19. (2)  State The term  State  means each State of the United States, the District of Columbia, and each commonwealth, territory, or possession of the United States.
