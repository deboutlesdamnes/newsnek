 1.  Short title  This Act may be cited as the  Keep Food Containers Safe from PFAS Act of 2021 .
 2.  Prohibition against food packaging containing intentionally added PFAS  (a)  In general Section 301 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act ( 21 U.S.C. 331 ) is amended by adding at the end the following: (fff)  (1)  The introduction or delivery for introduction into interstate commerce of food packaging containing intentionally added PFAS.  (2) The term  PFAS  means a perfluoroalkyl substance or a polyfluoroalkyl substance that is man-made with at least 1 fully fluorinated carbon atom. .  (b)  Applicability  The amendment made by subsection (a) applies beginning on January 1, 2024.
