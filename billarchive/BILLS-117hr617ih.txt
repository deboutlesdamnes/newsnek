 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  No Coronavirus Copays for Veterans Act .
 2.  Prohibition on copayments and cost sharing for veterans receiving preventive services relating to COVID–19  (a)  Prohibition The Secretary of Veterans Affairs may not require any copayment or other cost sharing under  chapter 17  of title 38, United States Code, for qualifying coronavirus preventive services. The requirement described in this subsection shall take effect with respect to a qualifying coronavirus preventive service on the specified date. (b)  Definitions In this section, the terms  qualifying coronavirus preventive service  and  specified date  have the meaning given those terms in section 3203 of the CARES Act ( Public Law 116–136 ).
