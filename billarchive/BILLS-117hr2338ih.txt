 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  District of Columbia National Guard Commanding General Residency Act .
 2.  Requirement that the commanding general of the District of Columbia National Guard reside in the District of Columbia  (a)  Requirement  The commanding general of the District of Columbia National Guard shall reside in the District of Columbia.  (b)  Effective date  Subsection (a) shall take effect on the day that is one year after the date of the enactment of this Act.
