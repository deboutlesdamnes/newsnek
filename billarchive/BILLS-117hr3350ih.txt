 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Small-business Procurement Utilization Reform Act  or the  SPUR Act .
 2.  Increase governmentwide small business procurement contract goal  (a)  In general Section 15(g)(1)(A)(i) of the Small Business Act ( 15 U.S.C. 644(g)(1)(A)(i) ) is amended by striking  23  and inserting  25 . (b)  Effective date  The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect on October 1, 2021.
