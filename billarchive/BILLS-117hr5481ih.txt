 1.  Name of Department of Veterans Affairs community-based outpatient clinic, Forest City, North Carolina  The Department of Veterans Affairs community-based outpatient clinic in Forest City, North Carolina, shall after the date of the enactment of this Act be known and designated as the  Corporal Jerry K. Crump VA Clinic . Any reference to such clinic in any law, regulation, map, document, record, or other paper of the United States shall be considered to be a reference to the Corporal Jerry K. Crump VA Clinic.
