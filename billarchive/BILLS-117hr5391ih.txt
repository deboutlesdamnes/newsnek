 1.  Prohibition on certain emergency temporary standards  (a)  In general Section 6(c) of the Occupational Safety and Health Act of 1970 ( 29 U.S.C. 655(c) ) is amended by adding at the end the following:  (4)  The Secretary may not issue an emergency temporary standard that requires an employer to ensure that employees of the employer receive a vaccine.  .  (b)  Application The amendment under subsection (a) shall not apply to any emergency temporary standard under section 6(c) of the Occupational Safety and Health Act of 1970 ( 29 U.S.C. 655(c) ) issued before the date of enactment of this Act.
