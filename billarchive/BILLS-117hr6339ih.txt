 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Health Coverage Tax Credit Reauthorization Act of 2021 . 
 2.  Extension of health coverage tax credit  (a)  In general  Section 35(b)(1)(B)  of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking  before January 1, 2022  and inserting  before January 1, 2026 . (b)  Effective date  The amendments made by this section shall apply to months beginning after December 31, 2021.
