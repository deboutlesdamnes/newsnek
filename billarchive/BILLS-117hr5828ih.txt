 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Pets Belong With Families Act . 
 2.  Prohibition on breed restrictions Section 31 of the United States Housing Act of 1937 ( 42 U.S.C. 1437z–3 ) is amended by striking subsection (b) and inserting the following: (b)  Reasonable requirements  (1)  In general  The reasonable requirements referred to in subsection (a) may include—  (A)  limitations on the number of animals in a unit, based on unit size;   (B)  prohibitions on types of animals that are classified as dangerous, provided that classification is based on specific behaviors or actions by the animal and is not based on the breed of the animal; and   (C)  restrictions or prohibitions based on size and type of building or project, or other relevant conditions.  (2)  Limitation  The reasonable requirements referred to in subsection (a) may not include prohibitions on breeds of animals.  .
