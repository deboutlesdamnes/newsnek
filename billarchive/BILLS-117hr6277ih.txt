 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Ethylene is Essential for Chemistry and Manufacturing Act . 
 2.  Excluding ethylene from taxation under Superfund excise tax  (a)  In general The table contained in  section 4661(b)  of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking the row relating to ethylene. (b)  Effective date  The amendment made by this section shall take effect on July 1, 2022.
