 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Telehealth Expansion Act of 2021 .
 2.  Exemption for telehealth services  (a)  In general  Section 223(c)(2)(E)  of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking  In the case of plan years beginning on or before December 31, 2021, a plan  and inserting  A plan . (b)  Certain coverage disregarded  Section 223(c)(1)(B)(ii)  of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking  (in the case of plan years beginning on or before December 31, 2021) .  (c)  Effective date  The amendments made by this section shall take effect on the date of the enactment of this Act.
