 1.  Harriet Tubman Post Office Building  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 501 Charles Street in Beaufort, South Carolina, shall be known and designated as the  Harriet Tubman Post Office Building . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Harriet Tubman Post Office Building .
