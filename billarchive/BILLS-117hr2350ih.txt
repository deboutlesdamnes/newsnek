 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Discriminatory Gaming Tax Repeal Act of 2021 .
 2.  Repeal  (a)  In general  Chapter 35 of subtitle D of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is repealed.  (b)  Effective date  The amendment made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2020.
