 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Emancipation Statue Removal Act . 
 2.  Removal of Emancipation Memorial  (a)  Removal  The Secretary of the Interior shall ensure that the Emancipation Memorial is removed from Lincoln Park in the District of Columbia.  (b)  Donation  The Secretary shall donate the Emancipation Memorial to a museum or other similar entity. The recipient of the Emancipation Memorial may not store, display, or exhibit the Emancipation Memorial outside. If the Emancipation Memorial is stored, displayed, or exhibited outside, ownership of the Emancipation Memorial will revert back to the Federal Government.
