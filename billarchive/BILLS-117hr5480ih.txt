 1. Applicability of Religious Freedom Restoration Act of  1993 The Religious Freedom Restoration Act of 1993 ( 42 U.S.C. 2000bb et seq. ) shall apply with any rule issued by the Secretary of Labor under the Occupational Safety and Health Act of 1970 ( 29 U.S.C. 651 et seq. ).
 2.  Prohibition on certain emergency temporary standards Section 6(c) of the Occupational Safety and Health Act of 1970 ( 29 U.S.C. 655(c) ) is amended by adding at the end the following:  (4)  The Secretary may not issue an emergency temporary standard that requires an employer to ensure that employees of the employer receive a vaccine.  .
