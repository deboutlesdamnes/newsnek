 1.  Designation of Lance Corporal Dana Cornell Darnell Outpatient Clinic  (a)  Designation The outpatient clinic of the Department of Veterans Affairs in Greenville, South Carolina, shall after the date of the enactment of this Act be known and designated as the  Lance Corporal Dana Cornell Darnell Outpatient Clinic . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Lance Corporal Dana Cornell Darnell Outpatient Clinic .
