 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Reducing Fraud in Unemployment Assistance Act . 
 2.  Information sharing requirement  (a)  In general Section 303 of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 503 ) is amended by adding at the end the following: (n)  For purposes of subsection (a), the unemployment compensation law of a State must provide that the State agency charged with the administration of the State law shallâ€”  (1)  enter into an agreement with the Attorney General under which the list of individuals receiving unemployment compensation under the State law shall be regularly compared with a list of each prisoner in Federal custody at a Federal correction facility within that State;  (2)  establish a regular comparison of such list of individuals with a list of each prisoner in the custody of that State at a correctional facility in that State; and  (3)  use the results of each such comparison to investigate and prosecute fraud, waste, and abuse of unemployment compensation under the State law.  .
