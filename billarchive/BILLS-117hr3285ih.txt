 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the “ 21st Century President Act ”.
 2.  Modernization of terms relating to the President and the spouse of a President Section 879(b)(1)(A) of title 18, United States Code, is amended by striking  the wife of a former President during his lifetime, the widow of a former President until her death or remarriage  and inserting  the spouse of a former President during a former President’s lifetime, the surviving spouse of a former President until the surviving spouse’s death or remarriage .
