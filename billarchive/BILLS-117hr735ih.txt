 1.  Arthur Luis Ibleto Post Office Building  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 502 East Cotati Avenue in Cotati, California, shall be known and designated as the  Arthur Luis Ibleto Post Office Building . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Arthur Luis Ibleto Post Office Building .
