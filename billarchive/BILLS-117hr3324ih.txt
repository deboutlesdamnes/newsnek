 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Incentivize Residential Energy Efficiency Act of 2021 .
 2.  Nonbusiness energy property credit made permanent  (a)  In general  Section 25C  of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking subsection (g). (b)  Effective date  The amendment made by this section shall apply to property placed in service after December 31, 2021.
