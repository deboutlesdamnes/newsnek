 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Retain Skilled Veterans Act .
 2.  Limitation on appointment of retired members of the Armed Forces to certain positions in the Department of Defense  (a)  In general  Section 3326 of title 5, United States Code, is amended—  (1) in the section heading, by inserting  certain  before  positions ; and  (2)  in subsection (b)—  (A) by striking  appointed  and all that follows through  Defense  and inserting  appointed to a position in the excepted or competitive service classified at or above GS–14 of the General Schedule (or equivalent) in or under the Department of Defense ; and (B) in paragraph (1), by striking  for the purpose  and all that follows through  Management . (b)  Clerical amendment The table of sections at the beginning of subchapter I of chapter 33 of such title is amended in the item relating to section 3326 by inserting  certain  before  positions .
