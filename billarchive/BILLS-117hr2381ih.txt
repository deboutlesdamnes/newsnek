 1.  Short title  (a)  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Protecting the Silos & Smokestacks National Heritage Area Act .
 2.  Funding authorization and cap  (a)  Funding authorization Section 707 of division II of the Omnibus Parks and Public Lands Management Act of 1996 ( Public Law 104–333 ) is amended by striking  September 30, 2021  and inserting  September 30, 2036 . (b)  Total funding cap Section 708(a) of division II of the Omnibus Parks and Public Lands Management Act of 1996 ( Public Law 104–333 ) is amended by striking the last sentence.
 3.  Redesignation of heritage area  (a)  Redesignation The America’s Agricultural Heritage Partnership established by section 703 of the Omnibus Parks and Public Lands Management Act of 1996 ( 16 U.S.C. 461  note;  Public Law 104–333 ) is hereby redesignated and shall be known as the  Silos & Smokestacks National Heritage Area . (b)  References  Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the heritage area referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Silos & Smokestacks National Heritage Area.
