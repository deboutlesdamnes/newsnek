 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  No COVID–19 Vaccination Mandate Act of 2021 .
 2.  No mandates for vaccination against COVID–19  Notwithstanding any Federal or State law, no institution or other entity, including any such Federal, State, local, Tribal, or private entity, may establish, implement, or enforce any mandate (including as a condition of employment, enrollment or attendance at school, or receipt of services) that any individual be vaccinated against SARS–Cov–2 or COVID–19.
