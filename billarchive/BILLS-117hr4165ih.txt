 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Uplifting First-Time Homebuyers Act of 2021 .
 2.  Increasing the limitation on qualified first-time homebuyer distributions  (a)  In general  Section 72(t)(8)(B)(i)  of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking  $10,000  and inserting  $20,000 . (b)  Effective date  The amendment made by this Act shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2020.
