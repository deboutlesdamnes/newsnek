 1.  General Tran Hung Dao Post Office  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 9317 Bolsa Avenue, in Westminster, California, shall be known and designated as the  General Tran Hung Dao Post Office . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  General Tran Hung Dao Post Office .
