 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  American Freedom and Internet Access Act of 2021 .
 2.  Operation starfall Not later than 45 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of the Air Force, in consultation with the Chief of Space Operations, shall develop and begin implementation of  Operation Starfall , a strategic plan to deploy stratospheric balloons, aerostats, or satellite technology capable of rapidly delivering wireless internet anywhere on the planet from the stratosphere or higher.
