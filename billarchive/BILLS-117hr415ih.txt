 1.  Agency required to pay attorney’s fees and costs in unsuccessful appeals  (a)  In general  Chapter 7  of title 5, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following: 707.  Attorney’s fees and costs in appeals  In the case that an agency appeals a ruling of a court under this chapter, and does not prevail in the appeal, the court shall award reasonable attorney’s fees and costs to the prevailing party. Such award shall be paid out of the administrative budget of the office in the agency that filed the appeal. 
 .  (b)  Clerical amendment The table of sections for  chapter 7  of title 5 is amended by inserting after the item related to section 706 the following: 707. Attorney’s fees and costs in appeals.  .
