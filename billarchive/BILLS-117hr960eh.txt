
 1.  John H. Leahr and Herbert M. Heilbrun Post Office 
 (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 3493 Burnet Avenue in Cincinnati, Ohio, shall be known and designated as the  John H. Leahr and Herbert M. Heilbrun Post Office .  (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  John H. Leahr and Herbert M. Heilbrun Post Office .
