 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Cancel the Coin Act .
 2.  Limitation on face value of coins  Section 5112 of title 31, United States Code, is amended—  (1)  in subsection (k)—  (A) by inserting  having such nominal, or face, values as the Secretary may determine but not in any case exceeding $200  after  platinum coins ; and (B) by striking  denominations, ; and (2)  by adding at the end the following new subsection:  (bb)  Limitation on face value  The Secretary may not mint or issue any coin having a nominal, or face, value exceeding $200.  .
