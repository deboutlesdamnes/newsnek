
 1.  Reducing child abuse and neglect due to the substance use disorder of a parent or caregiver Section 105(a) of the Child Abuse Prevention and Treatment Act ( 42 U.S.C. 5106(a) ) is amended by adding at the end the following:    (8)  Reducing child abuse and neglect due to the substance use disorder of a parent or caregiver  The Secretary may award grants under this subsection to entities to carry out activities to reduce child abuse and neglect due to the substance use disorder of a parent or caregiver.  .
