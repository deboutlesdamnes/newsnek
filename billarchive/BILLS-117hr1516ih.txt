 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Stop Foreign Interference in Ballot Measures Act .
 2.  Prohibition on contributions and donations by foreign nationals in connection with ballot initiatives and referenda  (a)  In general Section 319(a)(1)(A) of the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971 ( 52 U.S.C. 30121(a)(1)(A) ) is amended by striking  election;  and inserting the following:  election, including a State or local ballot initiative or referendum; .  (b)  Effective date  The amendment made by this Act shall apply with respect to elections held in 2022 or any succeeding year.
