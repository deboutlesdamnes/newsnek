 1.  Termination of certain executive actions  (a)  Termination  The following shall be void beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act:  (1)  Executive Orders 13986 and 13993.  (2)  Presidential Proclamation 10142.  (b)  Effect  All actions taken pursuant to any proclamation or Executive order terminated under subsection (a) shall cease on the date of the enactment of this Act.
