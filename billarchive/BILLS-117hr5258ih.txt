 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Improved Access to Trade Adjustment Assistance Act .
 2.  Modification of group eligibility requirements under trade adjustment assistance for workers program  (a)  In general Section 222(a)(2)(A)(i) of the Trade Act of 1974 ( 19 U.S.C. 2272(a)(2)(A)(i) ) is amended by adding at the end before the semicolon the following:  , failed to increase, or will decrease absolutely due to a scheduled or imminently anticipated, long-term decrease in or reallocation of the production capacity of the firm . (b)  Effective date The amendment made by subsection (a) applies with respect to petitions filed under section 221 of the Trade Act of 1974 ( 19 U.S.C. 2271 ) on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
