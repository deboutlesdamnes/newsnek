
 1.  PFC James Anderson, Jr., Post Office Building 
 (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 101 South Willowbrook Avenue in Compton, California, shall be known and designated as the  PFC James Anderson, Jr., Post Office Building .  (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  PFC James Anderson, Jr., Post Office Building .
