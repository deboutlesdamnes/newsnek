
 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Banning Lobbying And Safeguarding Trust Act  or the  BLAST Act .
  2.  Prohibiting former Members and officers of Congress from lobbying Congress   (a)  Prohibition  Section 207(e)(1) of title 18, United States Code, is amended to read as follows: 

 (1)  Members and elected officers of Congress  Any person who is a Senator, a Member of the House of Representatives, or an elected officer of the Senate or the House of Representatives and who, after that person leaves office, knowingly makes, with the intent to influence, any communication to or appearance before any Member, officer, or employee of either House of Congress or any employee of any other legislative office of the Congress, on behalf of any other person (except the United States) in connection with any matter on which such former Senator, Member, or elected official seeks action by a Member, officer, or employee of either House of Congress, in his or her official capacity, shall be punished as provided in section 216 of this title.  .
