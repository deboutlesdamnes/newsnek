 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Protection Against Double Tolling Act . 
 2.  Limitation on use of revenue  Section 129(a)(3)(A) of title 23, United States Code, is amended by striking clause (v) and inserting the following:  (v)  if the public authority certifies annually that the tolled facility is being adequately maintained, a toll rebate toward a congestion pricing fee for individuals residing on either side of a tolled bridge required to pay a toll to connect to the central business district in the city in which the vehicle of the individual is registered.  .
