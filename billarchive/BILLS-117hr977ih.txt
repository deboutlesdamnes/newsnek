 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Lady Liberty Act of 2021 .
 2.  Minimum number of refugees to be admitted Section 207(a) of the Immigration and Nationality Act ( 8 U.S.C. 1157(a) ) is amended by inserting after  in any fiscal year after fiscal year 1982 shall be such number as the President determines,  the following:  except that in any fiscal year after fiscal year 2022, such number may not be less than 125,000, without regard to whether or not the President makes any determination .
