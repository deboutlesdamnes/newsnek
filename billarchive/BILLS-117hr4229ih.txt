 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  National Sexual Assault Hotline Grants Reauthorization Act . 
 2.  Reauthorization of grants for the RAINN national sexual assault hotline and the RAINN national sexual assault online hotline Section 628(d) of the Adam Walsh Child Protection and Safety Act of 2006 ( 34 U.S.C. 20985(d) ) is amended by striking  fiscal years 2007 through 2010  and inserting  fiscal years 2022 through 2028 .
