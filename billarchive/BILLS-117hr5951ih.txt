 1.  Coya Knutson Post Office  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 202 2nd Avenue, in Oklee, Minnesota, shall be known and designated as the  Coya Knutson Post Office . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Coya Knutson Post Office .
