 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Downwinders Parity Act of 2021 .
 2.  Inclusion under the radiation exposure compensation act Section 4(b)(1) of the Radiation Exposure Compensation Act ( 42 U.S.C. 2210  note;  Public Law 101–426 ) is amended— (1)  in subparagraph (B)—  (A) by striking  that portion of ; and (B) by striking  that consists of townships 13 through 16 at ranges 63 through 71 ; and (2) in subparagraph (C), by inserting  all acreage in any county all or part of which is located in  before  that part .
 3.  Report  Within 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Attorney General shall submit to the relevant committees of the House of Representatives and of the Senate a report that outlines efforts to educate and conduct outreach to persons made newly eligible for benefits under the amendments made by section 2 of this Act.
