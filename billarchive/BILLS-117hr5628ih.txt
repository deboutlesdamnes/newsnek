 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Pecos Watershed Protection Act .
 2.  Withdrawal of Federal land in Pecos Watershed area, New Mexico  (a)  Definition of Federal land In this section, the term  Federal land  means the Federal land depicted as  Pecos Withdrawal  on the map entitled  Pecos Watershed, upstream of I–25 Mineral Withdrawal  and dated September 1, 2020.  (b)  Withdrawal  Subject to valid rights in existence on the date of enactment of this Act, the Federal land is withdrawn from all forms of—  (1)  entry, appropriation, or disposal under the public land laws;  (2)  location, entry, and patent under the mining laws; and   (3)  disposition under all laws pertaining to mineral and geothermal leasing or mineral materials.
