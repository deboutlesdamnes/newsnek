 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Helen Keller National Center Reauthorization Act of 2021 .
 2.  Helen Keller National Center Reauthorized The first sentence of section 205(a) of the Helen Keller National Center Act ( 29 U.S.C. 1904(a) ) is amended by striking  1999 through 2003  and inserting  2021 through 2025 .
