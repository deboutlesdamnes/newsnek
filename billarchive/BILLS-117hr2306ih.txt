 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Small Community Transit Improvement Act of 2021 .
 2.  Apportionments  Section 5336(h)(3) of title 49, United States Code, is amended to read as follows:  (3)  of amounts not apportioned under paragraphs (1) and (2), 3.0 percent shall be apportioned to urbanized areas with populations of less than 200,000 in accordance with subsection (i);  .
