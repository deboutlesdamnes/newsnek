 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Reopen Small Businesses Safely Act .
 2.  Use of economic injury disaster loan proceeds for covered worker protection expenditures  (a)  In general Not later than 10 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Administrator of the Small Business Administration shall issue such rules or guidance necessary to ensure that a borrower of a loan made under section 7(b)(2) of the Small Business Act ( 15 U.S.C. 636(b)(2) ) may use the proceeds of such loan for covered worker protection expenditures (as defined in section 7A(a) of the Small Business Act).
