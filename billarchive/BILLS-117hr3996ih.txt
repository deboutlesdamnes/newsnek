 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Empowering Law Enforcement To Fight Sex Trafficking Demand Act of 2021 .
 2.  Additional authorized use of Byrne JAG funds Section 501(a)(1) of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 ( 34 U.S.C. 10152(a)(1) ) is amended by adding at the end the following:  (I)  Programs to combat human trafficking (including programs to reduce the demand for trafficked persons).  .
