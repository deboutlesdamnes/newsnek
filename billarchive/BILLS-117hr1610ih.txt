 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Rural Broadband Window of Opportunity Act .
 2.  Purpose  The purpose of this Act is to level the playing field for service providers participating in the Rural Digital Opportunity Fund that are disadvantaged by short construction seasons. 
 3.  Application Processing Prioritization With respect to the long-form applicants of the Rural Digital Opportunity Fund Phase I auction provided for in the Report and Order in the matter of Rural Digital Opportunity Fund and Connect America Fund adopted by the Federal Communications Commission (in this section referred to as the  Commission ) on December 7, 2020 (FCC 20â€“5), the Commission shall prioritize the timely processing of long-form applications and any subsequent paperwork (in this section referred to as the  applications ) submitted by the long-form applicants as follows: The Commission shall prioritize the timely processing of applications which are proposing to provide service in geographic areas which the Commission determines to have the shortest construction seasons (such as in areas characterized by long winters with heavy snowfall).
