
 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  We’re Not Paying You To Break Our Laws Act .
  2.  Prohibition on payment of settlements in certain cases No amounts may be made available from the Treasury for the payment of any settlement agreement filed in the United States District Court for the District of Arizona in A.I.I.L. v. Sessions, 4:19–CV–00481, or any substantially similar civil action brought by an individual as a result of an action taken to enforce the memorandum of the Attorney General entitled  Zero-Tolerance for Offenses Under 8 U.S.C. §1325(a) , and dated April 6, 2018.
