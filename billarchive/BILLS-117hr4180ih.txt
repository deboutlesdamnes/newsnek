 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Keeping Pets and Families Together Act . 
 2.  Grants to support microchipping of dogs and cats Section 28 of the Animal Welfare Act ( 7 U.S.C. 2158 ) is amended by striking subsection (d) and inserting the following: (d)  Grants To support microchipping of dogs and cats  The Secretary shall establish a program under which the Secretary will award grants to entities described in subsection (a)(2) for purposes of supporting the capability of such entities to provide for microchipping of dogs and cats in their care before allowing such dogs or cats to be recovered by their original owner, adopted by other individuals, or sold to a dealer.  (e)  Regulations  The Secretary shall promulgate regulations to carry out this section. Not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of subsection (d), the Secretary shall promulgate regulations to carry out such subsection.   .
