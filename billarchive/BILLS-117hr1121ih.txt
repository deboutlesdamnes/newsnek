 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Empowering Immigration Courts Act .
 2.  Contempt authority of immigration judges Section 240(b)(1) of the Immigration and Nationality Act ( 8 U.S.C. 1229a(b)(1) ) is amended by striking  (under regulations prescribed by the Attorney General) to sanction by civil money penalty any action (or inaction) in contempt of the judge's proper exercise of authority under this Act  and inserting  to sanction by fine contempt of its authority in accordance with section 401 of title 18, United States Code .
