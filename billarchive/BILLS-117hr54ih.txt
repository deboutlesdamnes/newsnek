 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Farewell to Unnecessary Energy Lifelines Reform Act of 2021  or the  FUEL Reform Act .
 2.  Repeal of Department of Agriculture bioenergy subsidy programs and other related subsidy programs Title IX of the Farm Security and Rural Investment Act of 2002 ( 7 U.S.C. 8101  et seq.) is repealed.
