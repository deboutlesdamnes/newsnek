 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Biodiesel Tax Credit Extension Act of 2021 .
 2.  Biodiesel and renewable diesel  (a)  Income tax credit  (1)  In general  Section 40A(g)  of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking  2022  and inserting  2025 . (2)  Effective date  The amendment made by this subsection shall apply to fuel sold or used after December 31, 2022.  (b)  Excise tax incentives  (1)  Termination  (A)  In general Section 6426(c)(6) of such Code is amended by striking  2022  and inserting  2025 . (B)  Payments Section 6427(e)(6)(B) of such Code is amended by striking  2022  and inserting  2025 . (2)  Effective date  The amendments made by this subsection shall apply to fuel sold or used after December 31, 2022.
