 1.  Deputy Sheriff Brian Ishmael Post Office  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 3045 Sacramento Street in Placerville, California, shall be known and designated as the  Deputy Sheriff Brian Ishmael Post Office . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Deputy Sheriff Brian Ishmael Post Office .
