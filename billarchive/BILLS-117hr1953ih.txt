 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  No Taxpayer-Funded Checks for Prisoners Act . 
 2. Incarcerated individuals precluded from  2021  recovery rebates (a)  In general  Section 6428B(c)  of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (as added by the American Rescue Plan Act of 2021) is amended by striking  and  at the end of paragraph (2), by redesignating paragraph (3) as paragraph (4), and by inserting after paragraph (2) the following new paragraph: (3)  any individual who was incarcerated in a Federal prison on the date of the enactment of the American Rescue Plan Act of 2021, and  .  (b)  Effective date  The amendments made by this section shall take effect as if included in the enactment of section 9601 of the American Rescue Plan Act of 2021.
