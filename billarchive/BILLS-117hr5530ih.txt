 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Preventing Youth Substance Abuse Act of 2021 . 
 2.  Substance abuse affected youth initiative There is authorized to be appropriated for each fiscal year 2022 through 2026 $16,000,000 to carry out an the initiative relating to youth affected by opioids, stimulants, and other substance abuse implemented using amounts appropriated pursuant to paragraph (1)(E) of the  Department of Justice—Office of Justice Programs—Juvenile Justice Programs  account of title II of division B of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2020 ( Public Law 116–93 ).
