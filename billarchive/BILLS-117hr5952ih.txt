 1.  Jon Glawe Post Office  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 123 East Main Street, in Vergas, Minnesota, shall be known and designated as the  Jon Glawe Post Office . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Jon Glawe Post Office .
