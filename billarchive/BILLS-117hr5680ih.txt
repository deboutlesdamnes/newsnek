 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Saving Workers by Eliminating Economic Tampering and Ensuring Reliability Act  or the  SWEETER Act .
 2.  Repeal of sugar program  (a)  In general Section 156 of the Federal Agriculture Improvement and Reform Act of 1996 ( 7 U.S.C. 7272 ) is repealed. (b)  Flexible marketing allotments for sugar and tariff rate quotas Part VII of the Agricultural Adjustment Act of 1938 ( 7 U.S.C. 1359aa et seq. ) is repealed. (c)  Feedstock flexibility program Section 9010 of the Farm Security and Rural Investment Act of 2002 ( 7 U.S.C. 8110 ) is repealed.
