 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Championing our Parents Act of 2021 .
 2.  Prohibition on use of Federal funds No Federal funds may be expended to implement the memorandum from the Attorney General entitled  Partnership among Federal, State, Local, Tribal, and Territorial Law Enforcement to address threats against School Administrators, Board Members, Teachers, and Staff  dated October 4, 2021.
