 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Fair Pay for All Act of 2021 .
 2.  Sex to include sexual orientation and gender identity  (a)  In general Section 3 of the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 ( 29 U.S.C. 203 ) is amended by adding at the end the following: (t)  (1)  Sex  includes— (A)  a sex stereotype;  (B)  pregnancy, childbirth, or a related medical condition;  (C)  sexual orientation or gender identity; and  (D)  sex characteristics, including intersex traits.  (2)  Sexual orientation  includes homosexuality, heterosexuality, and bisexuality. (3)  Gender identity  means the gender-related identity, appearance, mannerisms, or other gender-related characteristics of an individual, regardless of the individual’s designated sex at birth.  .  (b)  Sex-Based discrimination Section 6(d)(1) of the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 ( 29 U.S.C. 206(d)(1) ) is amended by striking  opposite  and inserting  another .
