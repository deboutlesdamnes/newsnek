 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Sustainable Agriculture Research Act .
 2.  Additional goal of AGARDA to enhance role of agriculture in innovative sustainability solutions Section 1473H(b)(2) of the National Agricultural Research, Extension, and Teaching Policy Act of 1977 ( 7 U.S.C. 3319k(b)(2) ) is amended— (1) in subparagraph (C), by striking  and  at the end; (2)  by redesignating subparagraph (D) as subparagraph (E); and  (3)  by inserting after subparagraph (C) the following:  (D)  to enhance the role of agriculture in innovative voluntary resilience solutions in the United States through the development of agricultural technologies that address—  (i)  the impact of extreme weather on crop production;  (ii)  the expansion of the potential for long-term carbon storage through agriculture;  (iii)  increased economic and practical feasibility for renewable and sustainable energy on farms and in the agriculture industry; and  (iv)  increased adoption of voluntary conservation practices that sequester carbon and build on-farm climate resilience; and  .
