 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Judiciary Act of 2021 .
 2.  Number of justices; quorum Section 1 of title 28, United States Code, is amended by striking  a Chief Justice of the United States and eight associate justices, any six of whom shall constitute a quorum  and inserting  a Chief Justice of the United States and twelve associate justices, any eight of whom shall constitute a quorum .
