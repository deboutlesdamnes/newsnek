 1.  John R. Hatcher III Post Office Building  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1961 North C Street in Oxnard, California, shall be known and designated as the  John R. Hatcher III Post Office Building . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  John R. Hatcher III Post Office Building .
