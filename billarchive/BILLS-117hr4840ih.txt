 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  End Misleading Cultural Exchanges Act .
 2.  Exclusion of Government of the People’s Republic of China from certain cultural exchanges Subsection (a) of section 108A of the Mutual Educational and Cultural Exchange Act of 1961 ( 22 U.S.C. 2458a(a) ) is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph: (3) For purposes of this section, the term  foreign government  does not include the Government of the People’s Republic of China.  .
