 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Gold Star Families Benefits Protection Act .
 2.  Extension of certain morale, welfare, and recreation privileges to certain recipients of the gold star lapel button or next of kin deceased personnel lapel button  (a)  In general  Chapter 54  of title 10, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new section: 1067.  Use of commissary stores and MWR facilities: certain recipients of the gold star lapel button or next of kin deceased personnel lapel button  (a)  Eligibility  A covered individual shall be permitted to use commissary stores and MWR retail facilities on the same basis as a member of the armed forces on active duty. (2) The term  MWR retail facilities  has the meaning given that term in section 1063 of this title.
