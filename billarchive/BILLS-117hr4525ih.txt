 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Supporting Work Through Apprenticeships Act .
 2.  Supporting work through apprenticeships Section 407(d)(5) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 607(d)(5) ) is amended by inserting  , including apprenticeship  before the semicolon.
 3.  Effective date  The amendment made by this Act shall take effect on October 1, 2022.
