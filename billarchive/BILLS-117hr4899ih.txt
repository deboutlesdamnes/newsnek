 1.  Neal Kenneth Todd Post Office  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 10 Broadway Street West, in Akeley, Minnesota, shall be known and designated as the  Neal Kenneth Todd Post Office . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Neal Kenneth Todd Post Office .
