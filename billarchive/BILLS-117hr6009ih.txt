 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Pandemic Cessation Act . 
 2.  Termination of COVID–19 public health emergency  (a)  In general The public health emergency declared pursuant to section 319 of the Public Health Service Act ( 42 U.S.C. 247d ) on January 31, 2020, with respect to COVID–19, and any renewal thereof, shall terminate on the first date after the date of enactment of this Act on which the Secretary of Health and Human Services— (1) grants approval under section 505 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act ( 21 U.S.C. 355 ) to an application with respect to a new drug intended to treat COVID–19; or (2) authorizes emergency use under section 564 of such Act ( 21 U.S.C. 360bbb–3 ) with respect to a drug intended to treat COVID–19. (b)  Definition In this section, the terms  drug  and  new drug  have the meanings given such terms in section 201 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act ( 21 U.S.C. 321 ).
