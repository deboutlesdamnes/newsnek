 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Improving College Affordability for our Guard and Reserve Act .
 2.  Definition of independent student Section 480(d) of the Higher Education Act of 1965 ( 20 U.S.C. 1087vv(d) ) is amended— (1)  in paragraph (1)—  (A)  by redesignating subparagraphs (E) through (I) as subparagraphs (F) through (J), respectively; and  (B)  by striking subparagraph (D) and inserting the following:  (D)  is a veteran of the Armed Forces (as defined in subsection (c)(1));  (E)  is a member of the Armed Forces (including the National Guard or Reserve) without regard to whether such individual is serving on active duty;  ; and  (2) in paragraph (2), by striking  paragraph (1)(I)  and inserting  paragraph (1)(J) .
