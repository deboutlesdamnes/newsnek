 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Railway Upgrades for Rural American Lines Act  or the  RURAL Act .
 2.  Railway-highway crossings program  Section 130 of title 23, United States Code, is amended—  (1)  in subsection (e)(1)—  (A) in subparagraph (A) by striking  and the installation of protective devices  and inserting  , the installation of protective devices, and the replacement of functionally obsolete warning devices ; and (B) in subparagraph (B) by striking  the installation of protective devices at railway-highway crossings  and inserting  construction or installation of protective devices (including replacement of functionally obsolete protective devices) at railway-highway crossings ;  (2) in subsection (f)(3) by striking  90 percent  and inserting  100 percent ; and (3) in subsection (i)(3)(B) by striking  $7,500  and inserting  $100,000 .
