 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Closing Loopholes for Oil and other Sources of Emissions Act  or the  CLOSE Act .
 2.  Repeal of exemption for aggregation of emissions from oil and gas sources Section 112(n) of the Clean Air Act ( 42 U.S.C. 7412(n) ) is amended by striking paragraph (4).
