 1.  Marine Corps Reserve PVT Jacob Cruz Post Office  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 2016 East 1st Street in Los Angeles, California, shall be known and designated as the  Marine Corps Reserve PVT Jacob Cruz Post Office . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Marine Corps Reserve PVT Jacob Cruz Post Office .
