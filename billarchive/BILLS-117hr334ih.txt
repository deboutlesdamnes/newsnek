 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Insurrection Financing Transparency Act of 2021 . 
 2.  Law enforcement authority  (a)  In general  A law enforcement agency may require a reporting company to provide the agency with a report that contains the information described in section 5336(b)(2) of title 31, United States Code, if the law enforcement agency certifies to the reporting company that the report is required to carry out the agencyâ€™s investigation into the January 6th attack on the U.S. Capitol.  (b)  Penalties  The penalties provided under section 5336(h) of title 31, United States Code, shall apply to a violation of this section to the same extent as they apply to a violation of such section 5336.  (c)  Definitions  The definitions provided in section 5336(a) of title 31, United States Code, apply to this section.
