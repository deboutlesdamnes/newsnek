 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Flood Insurance Fairness Act .
 2.  Chargeable premium rates Notwithstanding any other provision of law, including section 1308 of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 ( 42 U.S.C. 4015 ), with respect to the chargeable premium rates described in subsection (a)(1) of that section, as in effect on the date of enactment of this Act, the Administrator of the Federal Emergency Management Agency may not adjust those rates unless a duly enacted law of the United States enacted after the date of enactment of this Act provides the Administrator with that authority.
