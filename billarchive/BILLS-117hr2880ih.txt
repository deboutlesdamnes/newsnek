 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Vessel Response Plan Improvement Act .
 2.  Search and rescue notification requirements for vessel response plans of Coast Guard-approved commercial vessels  (a)  In general  The Commandant of the Coast Guard shall modify subparts I and J of part 155 of title 33, Code of Federal Regulations, to require the vessel response plans of Coast Guard-approved tank and nontank commercial vessels to include the procedures described in subsection (b) for use by the Coast Guard in the case of a search and rescue mission.  (b)  Procedures  The procedures described in this subsection are as follows:  (1)  A procedure for providing, not less frequently than twice daily for the duration of a search and rescue mission, notifications to family members of the crew of the vessel with respect to the status of the search and rescue mission, any injured crew member, and salvage.
