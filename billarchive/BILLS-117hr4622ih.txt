 1.  Ronald E. Rosser Post Office  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 226 North Main Street in Roseville, Ohio, shall be known and designated as the  Ronald E. Rosser Post Office . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Ronald E. Rosser Post Office .
