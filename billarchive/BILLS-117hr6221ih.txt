 1.  James A. Rogers Jr. Post Office  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 155 Main Avenue West in Winsted, Minnesota, shall be known and designated as the  James A. Rogers Jr. Post Office . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  James A. Rogers Jr. Post Office .
