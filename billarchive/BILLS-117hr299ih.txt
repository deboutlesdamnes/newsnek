 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Noncontiguous Shipping Reasonable Rate Act of 2021 .
 2.  Noncontiguous domestic ocean trade  Section 13701(d) of title 49, United States Code, is amended by striking paragraph (1) and inserting the following new paragraph:  (1)  In general  For purposes of this section, a rate is reasonable if such rate is within 10 percent of a rate set by a comparable international ocean rate index recognized by the Federal Maritime Commission.  .
