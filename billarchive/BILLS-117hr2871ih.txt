 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Resist Executive Amnesty on Defense Installations Act  or the  READI Act .
 2.  Prohibition on use of military installations to house aliens who do not have a lawful immigration status or are undergoing removal proceedings in the United States  (a)  Prohibition  A military installation may not be used to house any alien unlawfully present in the United States.  (b)  Definitions  In this section:  (1) The term  alien unlawfully present  means an alien whoâ€” (A) is covered by the rule of construction regarding unlawful presence contained in section 212(a)(9)(B)(ii) of the Immigration and Nationality Act ( 8 U.S.C. 1182(a)(9)(B)(ii) ); or (B)  is undergoing removal proceedings in the United States.  (2) The term  military installation  has the meaning given that term in section 2801(c)(4) of title 10, United States Code, but does not include an installation located outside of the United States.
