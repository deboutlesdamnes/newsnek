 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Stop Hate Crimes Act of 2021 .
 2.  Clarification as to causation element in hate crimes  Section 249(a) of title 18, United States Code, is amended—  (1)  in paragraph (1), in the matter preceding subparagraph (A)—  (A) by striking  because of  and inserting  if ; and (B) by inserting after  origin of any person  the following:  was a contributory motivating factor for causing or attempting to cause such injury ; and (2)  in paragraph (2)(A), in the matter preceding clause (i)—  (A) by striking  because of  and inserting  if ; and (B) by inserting after  disability of any person  the following:  was a contributory motivating factor for causing or attempting to cause such injury .
