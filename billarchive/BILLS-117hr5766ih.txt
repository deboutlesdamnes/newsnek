 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Helicopter Data and Voice Recorder Requirement Act .
 2.  Helicopter recorders  (a)  In general  Chapter 447  of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following: 44744.  Helicopter recorders  It shall be unlawful for any air carrier to operate a single or multiengine, turbine-powered helicopter with 1 or more passenger seats and 1 or more pilots without a flight data recorder and cockpit voice recorder that meet the requirements of section 91.609 of title 14, Code of Federal Regulations. 
 .  (b)  Clerical amendment The analysis for  chapter 447  of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following: 44744. Helicopter recorders.   .  (c)  Effective date  The amendments made by this section shall take effect on the date that is 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act.
