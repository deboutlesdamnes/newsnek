 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Cameras in the Courtroom Act .
 2.  Amendment to title 28  (a)  In general  Chapter 45  of title 28, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following: 678.  Televising Supreme Court proceedings  The Supreme Court shall permit television coverage of all open sessions of the Court unless the Court decides, by a vote of the majority of justices, that allowing such coverage in a particular case would constitute a violation of the due process rights of one or more of the parties before the Court. 
 .  (b)  Clerical amendment The chapter analysis for  chapter 45  of title 28, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following: 678. Televising Supreme Court proceedings.  .
