 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Blue Ridge National Heritage Area Reauthorization Act of 2021 .
 2.  Reauthorization of the Blue Ridge National Heritage Area The Blue Ridge National Heritage Area Act of 2003 ( 54 U.S.C. 320101  note;  Public Law 108–108 ; 117 Stat. 1274; 131 Stat. 461; 132 Stat. 661; 133 Stat. 778) is amended— (1)  in subsection (i)—  (A)  by striking paragraph (1) and inserting the following:  (1)  In general  There is authorized to be appropriated to carry out this section $1,000,000 for each fiscal year.  ; and  (B) in paragraph (2), by striking  subsection (a)  and inserting  paragraph (1) ; and (2) in subsection (j), by striking  2021  and inserting  2036 .
