 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Veterans Health Flexibility Act .
 2.  Expansion of eligibility for non-Department of Veterans Affairs medical care under Veterans Community Care Program  Section 1703 of title 38, United States Code, is amended—  (1)  by striking subsection (d) and inserting the following new subsection (d):  (d)  Conditions under which care is required To be furnished through non-Department providers  Subject to the availability of appropriations, the Secretary shall furnish hospital care, medical services, or extended care services to a covered veteran through health care providers specified in subsection (c), if the covered veteran notifies the Secretary that the veteran desires to receive care through the Veterans Community Care Program.  ;  (2)  by striking subsection (e);  (3)  by redesignating subsections (f) through (o) as subsections (e) through (n), respectively;  (4) in subsection (e), as so redesignated by striking  or (e) ; and (5)  in subsection (l)(2), as so redesignated—  (A) in subparagraph (A), by striking  subsection (i)  and inserting  subsection (h) ; (B)  by striking subparagraph (B); and  (C)  by redesignating subparagraphs (C) and (D) as subparagraphs (B) and (C), respectively.
