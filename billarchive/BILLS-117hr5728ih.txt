 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  JAB Act  or the  Justice for All Businesses Act . 
 2.  Prohibition of Funding for OSHA Vaccine-and-Testing Emergency Temporary Standard  No funds may be used by the Secretary of Labor to draft, promulgate, or enforce a rule mandating that employers require employees to be vaccinated against or tested for COVID–19. 
 3.  Prohibition on Assessing Penalties with Respect to OSHA Vaccine-and-Testing Emergency Temporary Standard The Secretary of Labor shall not assess civil monetary or criminal penalties under section 17 of the Occupational Safety and Health Act ( 29 U.S.C. 666 ) for violations of a rule mandating that employers require employees to be vaccinated against or tested for COVID–19.
