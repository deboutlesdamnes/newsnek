 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Accelerating Holding Foreign Companies Accountable Act . 
 2.  Trading prohibition for 2 consecutive non-inspection years Section 104(i) of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002 ( 15 U.S.C. 7214(i) ) is amended— (1) in paragraph (2)(A)(ii), by striking  the foreign jurisdiction described in clause (i)  and inserting  a foreign jurisdiction ; and  (2)  in paragraph (3)—  (A) in the paragraph heading, by striking  3  and inserting  2 ; and (B) in subparagraph (A), in the matter preceding clause (i), by striking  3  and inserting  2 .
