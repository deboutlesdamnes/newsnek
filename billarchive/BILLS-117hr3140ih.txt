 2.  12-year limit for occupying position of Director of National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases  (a)  In general  Notwithstanding section 405(a)(2) of the Public Health Service Act, an individual may not serve as Director of the National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases for a period greater than 12 total years.
