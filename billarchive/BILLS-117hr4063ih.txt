 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Barack Obama Highway Act .
 2.  Designation The portion of Interstate Route 57 that is located in Illinois shall be known and designated as the  Barack Obama Highway .
 3.  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the portion of Interstate Route 57 referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Barack Obama Highway .
