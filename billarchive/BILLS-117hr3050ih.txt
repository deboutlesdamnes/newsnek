 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Kenneth Meisel Public Servants’ Claimant Fairness Act . 
 2.  Increase in time-period for FECA claimant to supply supporting documentation to Office of Worker’s Compensation  Not later than 60 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Labor shall—  (1) amend section 10.121 of title 20, Code of Federal Regulations, by striking  30 days  and inserting  60 days ; and (2)  modify the Federal Employees Compensation Act manual to reflect the changes to such section made by the Secretary pursuant to paragraph (1).
