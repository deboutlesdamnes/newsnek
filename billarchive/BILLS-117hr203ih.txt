 1.  Benny C. Martinez Post Office Building  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 4020 Broadway Street in Houston, Texas, shall be known and designated as the  Benny C. Martinez Post Office Building . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Benny C. Martinez Post Office Building .
