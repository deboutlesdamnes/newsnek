 1.  Designation of Maude Frazier Mountain in the State of Nevada  (a)  In general The peak of Frenchman Mountain located at latitude 36°10′45″ N, by longitude 114°59′52″ W in the State of Nevada shall be known and designated as  Maude Frazier Mountain . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, record, or other paper of the United States to the peak referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to  Maude Frazier Mountain .
