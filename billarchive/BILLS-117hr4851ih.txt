(b)  Holding SSI beneficiaries harmless  For purposes of determining the income of an individual to establish eligibility for, and the amount of, benefits payable under title XVI of the Social Security Act, the amount of any benefit to which the individual is entitled under title II of such Act shall be deemed not to exceed the amount of the benefit that would be determined for such individual under such title as in effect on the day before the date of the enactment of this Act.  (c)  Effective date  The amendments made by this section shall apply with respect to widow’s and widower’s insurance benefits payable for months after December 2021.
