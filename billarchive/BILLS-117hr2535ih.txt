 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Protecting Flores Agreement Act .
 2.  Flores settlement agreement No Federal funds may be used to violate the terms of the stipulated settlement agreement filed on January 17, 1997, in the United States District Court for the Central District of California in Flores v. Reno, CV 85–4544–RJK (commonly known as the  Flores settlement agreement ).
