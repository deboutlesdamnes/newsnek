 1.  Howard Arthur Tibbs Post Office  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 275 Penn Avenue in Salem, Ohio, shall be known and designated as the  Howard Arthur Tibbs Post Office . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Howard Arthur Tibbs Post Office .
