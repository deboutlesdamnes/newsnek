 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  No Wireless Installations in Federal Instruments for Voting Act  or the  No WIFI Act . 
 2.  Prohibiting voting systems from containing wireless components  (a)  Prohibition Section 301(a) of the Help America Vote Act of 2002 ( 52 U.S.C. 21081(a) ) is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph: (7)  Prohibiting wireless components  The voting system may not contain any wireless components.  .  (b)  Effective date  The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to elections held during 2022 or any succeeding year.
