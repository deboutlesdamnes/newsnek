 1.  Joseph R. Lentol Post Office  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 66 Meserole Avenue in Brooklyn, New York, shall be known and designated as the  Joseph R. Lentol Post Office . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Joseph R. Lentol Post Office .
