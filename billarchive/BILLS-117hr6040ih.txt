 1.  Robert Smalls Post Office  (a)  Designation The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 11 Robert Smalls Parkway Suite C, in Beaufort, South Carolina, shall be known and designated as the  Robert Smalls Post Office . (b)  References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the  Robert Smalls Post Office .
