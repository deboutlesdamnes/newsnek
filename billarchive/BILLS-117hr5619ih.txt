 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Marginal Well Protection Act .
 2.  Methane fee from petroleum and natural gas systems  Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the owner or operator of a facility that produces less than 15 barrels of oil per day and less than 90 Mcf of natural gas per day shall not be subject to any fee imposed by the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency in connection with reporting methane emissions pursuant to subpart W of part 98 of title 40, Code of Federal Regulations (or any successor regulations).
