(b)  Penalties for false certification  Section 3704(c) of such title is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:  (3)  Any veteran who knowingly and materially makes a false certification under paragraph (1) or (2) of this subsection shall be liable to the United States Government for a civil penalty in an amount not to exceed $23,607. A penalty under this paragraph may be in addition to any other remedy available to the Secretary. All determinations necessary to carry out this paragraph shall be made by the Secretary.  .  (c)  Loan fees The loan fee table in section 3729(b)(2) is amended by striking  April 7, 2023  each place it appears and inserting  April 7, 2027 . (d)  Effective date  The amendments made by this Act shall take effect on the date that is 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act.
