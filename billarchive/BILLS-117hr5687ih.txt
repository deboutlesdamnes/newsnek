 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Backing the Independent Decisions of Employees against Nefarious Mandates Act of 2021 .
 2.  Civil action for COVID–19 vaccine-related injury or death An individual may bring an action in an appropriate district court of the United States against their employer seeking damages and injunctive relief if the employee suffered a vaccine-related injury or death (as such term is defined in section 2133(5) of the Public Health Service Act ( 42 U.S.C. 300aa–33(5) )) as a result of receiving a COVID–19 vaccine, which vaccine the individual received only as a result of a requirement to do so by the employer.
