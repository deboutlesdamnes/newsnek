 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Nicaragua Free Trade Review Act of 2021 .
 2.  Report on compliance of Nicaragua with the Dominican Republic-Central America-United States Free Trade Agreement  Not later than 60 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the United States Trade Representative shall submit to Congress a report on the manner and extent to which Nicaragua is in compliance with the Dominican Republic-Central America-United States Free Trade Agreement, signed at Washington, DC, on August 5, 2004, and entered into effect on March 1, 2006.
