 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Sunshine Forever Act .
 2.  Extension of solar energy credit  (a)  In general  Section 48(a)(2)(A)(i)(II)  of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking  January 1, 2024  and inserting  January 1, 2035 . (b)  Repeal of phaseout  Section 48(a) of such Code is amended by striking paragraph (6).  (c)  Effective date  The amendments made by this section shall apply to property the construction of which begins after December 31, 2020.
