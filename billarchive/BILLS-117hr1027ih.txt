 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Safe Bases Act .
 2.  Permits to carry concealed firearms on military installations  (a)  In general  Not later than one year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Defense shall establish a single element of the Department of Defense to issue, upon request by a covered member, a permit that authorizes that covered member to carry a concealed firearm while on any military installation.
