 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Common Sense Standards for Visa Applicants Act .
 2.  Legal effect of final rule  (a)  In general The final rule of the Department of Homeland Security entitled  Inadmissibility on Public Charge Grounds  (84 Fed. Reg. 41292, August 14, 2019) shall have the full force and effect of law. (b)  Conforming change The final rule of the Department of Homeland Security entitled  Inadmissibility on Public Charge Grounds; Implementation of Vacatur  (86 Fed. Reg. 14221, March 15, 2021) shall have no force or effect.
