 1.  Short title This Act may be cited as the  Home Loan Quality Transparency Act of 2021 .
 2.  Exemption from certain disclosure requirements  (a)  In general Section 304 of the Home Mortgage Disclosure Act of 1975 ( 12 U.S.C. 2803 ) is amended— (1)  by striking subsection (i) and inserting the following:  (i)  Exemption from certain disclosure requirements  The requirements of paragraphs (4), (5), and (6) of subsection (b) shall not apply with respect to any depository institution described in section 303(3)(A) that has total assets, as of the most recent full fiscal year of the institution, of $30,000,000 or less.  ; and  (2)  by striking subsection (o).  (b)  Technical and conforming amendment Section 104 of the Economic Growth, Regulatory Relief, and Consumer Protection Act ( Public Law 115–174 ; 132 Stat. 1301) is amended by striking subsection (b).
